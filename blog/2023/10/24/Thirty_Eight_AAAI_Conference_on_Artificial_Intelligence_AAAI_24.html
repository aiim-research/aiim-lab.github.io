Tutorial and Lab on Graph Counterfactual Explainability accepted to be held at AAAI 2024

October 24, 2023

We are delighted to announce that our proposals for both a tutorial and a lab have been accepted for presentation at the Thirty-Eighth AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI-24), scheduled to take place at the Vancouver Convention Centre in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, from February 20 to February 27, 2024.

As is widely recognized, the tutorial and lab program constitutes a pivotal component of the comprehensive conference agenda. In light of this, we are committed to exerting our utmost efforts to ensure the successful execution of these events. More info at tutorial and lab call.

To provide more specific details, the Tutorial and Lab Forum is scheduled to occur on Tuesday and Wednesday, specifically on February 20 and 21, 2024.

We cordially invite you not to miss our tutorial session titled

  • “Graphs Counterfactual Explainability: A Comprehensive Landscape” Website of the event

and the closely associated laboratory session named

  • “Digging into the Landscape of Graphs Counterfactual Explainability.” Website of the event

Keep an eye out for further updates!
