Invited talk XAI.it 2023 - 4th Italian Workshop on Explainable Artificial Intelligence | co-located with AIxIA 2023

November 01, 2023

Rome, Italy — The 4th Italian Workshop on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (AI), co-located with AIxIA 2023, is thrilled to announce the participation of a renowned expert in the field of Artificial Intelligence, G. Stilo, who has been invited as the keynote speaker for this prestigious event.

The workshop is set to take place on November 8, 2023, and promises to be an enlightening experience for all attendees. Stilo’s keynote address, titled “Graphs’ Counterfactual Explainability Landscape: Current State and Frontiers,” will be a focal point of the event. This keynote promises to shed light on an emerging and crucial aspect of AI research.

Counterfactual Explanation (CE) techniques have garnered considerable attention for their ability to provide insights to users interacting with AI systems. While these techniques have been extensively studied in domains such as medical imaging and autonomous vehicles, Graph Counterfactual Explanation (GCE) methods have remained relatively under-explored. GCEs offer a unique perspective by generating a new graph, similar to the original one, but with different outcomes based on the underlying predictive model.

During Stilo’s presentation, attendees will embark on a journey through the realm of GCE, starting with foundational concepts. The keynote will introduce a categorization of explainers and highlight key tools essential for those venturing into this field. Moreover, the audience will gain insights into the latest advancements in GCE, witness a visual comparison of various generative methods, and conclude with valuable takeaways and remarks.
