Everything is Ready for DELTA Cutting-Edge Workshop at KDD 2024!

August 20, 2024

As anticipation builds for KDD 2024, the data science and machine learning community is buzzing with excitement, and for good reason. One of the standout events at this year’s conference is the KDD Workshop on Discovering Drift Phenomena in Evolving Landscapes (DELTA 2024), set to take place on August 26, 2024, from 2:00 to 6:00 PM in room 120.

What’s in Store at DELTA 2024?

This workshop promises to be a must-attend event, especially for those passionate about the evolving challenges in drift detection and data streams. DELTA 2024 is packed with cutting-edge insights and lively discussions that are sure to leave attendees both inspired and well-informed about the latest advancements in the field.

Meet the Experts: Invited Speakers

DELTA 2024 boasts an impressive lineup of speakers featuring two of the prominent minds in the world of data streams and machine learning:

  • Heitor Murilo Gomes, senior lecturer at Victoria University of Wellington (VuW), New Zealand. He will be delivering a talk titled “Practical Challenges and Insights in Concept Drift Research.” Heitor Murilo Gomes is renowned for his pioneering work in ensemble learning, unsupervised drift detection, making his talk a highlight for those keen to learn about the latest techniques in handling data streams.

  • Joao Gama, Full Professor at the School of Economics, University of Porto, Portugal, will be sharing his expertise through a talk on “A Neuro-Symbolic Explainer for Rare Events.” Professor Gama’s research on neuro-symbolic methods provides valuable insights into the explanation and prediction of rare, yet critical, events within data streams.

Why You Should Attend

Whether you’re an experienced data scientist or just starting your journey in machine learning, DELTA 2024 is the perfect opportunity to deepen your understanding of drift phenomena in evolving landscapes. Attendees can expect a dynamic exchange of ideas, with opportunities to network with fellow professionals who share a passion for the latest trends and challenges in this rapidly evolving field.

Join Us in Barcelona!

If you’re attending KDD 2024 in Barcelona, make sure to mark your calendar for DELTA 2024. This workshop is not just an opportunity to learn from top experts, but also a chance to connect with a community of like-minded professionals. Stop by room 120, say hi, and immerse yourself in the cutting-edge world of machine learning and drift detection.

Don’t miss out on this exciting event at KDD 2024! For more details, check out the Program at a Glance and get ready to explore the future of data streams and drift detection.

See you in Barcelona!
