Invited talks at TU Munich

July 11, 2024

We are thrilled to announce that we will be visiting the Technical University of Munich (TUM) with my current PhD student, Andrea D’Angelo. During our visit, we will be collaborating with the Responsible Data Science group, under the esteemed leadership of Prof. Gjergji Kasneci.

This visit promises to be an incredible opportunity for intensive research and vibrant intellectual exchanges. We are particularly excited to deliver our two invited talks:

  • Prof. G. Stilo - “Bias, Fairness, and Explainability: Building Trust in AI Systems”
  • Dr. A. D’Angelo - “Machine Unlearning: Safeguarding Privacy and Security in AI Models”

We are looking forward to impactful and productive days ahead. Stay tuned for updates!
